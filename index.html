Overview

South Mumbai's First Ever Gated Development

Welcome to South Mumbai — the gateway to the city of dreams, where timeless charm meets modern aspirations. A place where vibrant city life blends seamlessly with refined living, offering an address that is both prestigious and well-connected. At the heart of this iconic destination stands Lodha Bellevue, Mahalaxmi, a landmark development that redefines luxury, comfort, and exclusivity. Designed with spacious layouts, natural light, and elegant finishes, it offers a truly elevated lifestyle in one of Mumbai’s most sought-after locations.

Lodha Bellevue offers premium residences across its iconic towers, with Tower 3 (G+37) featuring homes starting from ₹7 Cr and Tower 1 (G+57) offering residences starting from ₹11 Cr. With possession timelines of December 2026 and January 2027 respectively, the project ensures a well-planned delivery. A flexible payment plan of 25% at booking and 75% on OC adds convenience, making luxury living more accessible. Located in Mahalaxmi, it offers unmatched connectivity along with the prestige of a South Mumbai address.
